"Hollywood Regency" is an interior design style that dates back to the Golden Age of Hollywood in the 1920s to 1950s. This style is characterized by a blend of elegance, luxury, and boldness, with a focus on bright colors, bold patterns, and luxurious details such as glass, mirrors, crystal, and precious metals. The style features luxurious furniture that combines classic and modern styles, with plush velvet and leather, floral and geometric patterned fabrics. The style remains popular today,.
